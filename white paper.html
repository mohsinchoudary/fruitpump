Fruitpump (APB) — Whitepaper

The Angry Apple of Solana — short, clear intro and mission

1. Introduction

Fruitpump (APB) is a meme-driven, community-powered Solana token built around the idea of fun, virality, and simplicity. The project's mascot — the Angry Apple — symbolizes the pushback against “red candles” in the market and the community spirit that celebrates green growth.

2. Mission

To build the most fun and recognizable fruit-based meme brand on Solana by supporting community growth, providing engaging social campaigns, and keeping tokenomics simple and transparent.
